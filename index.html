Exellence & Integrity

Our school is committed to excellence and integrity, fostering a learning environment that upholds the highest academic standards, ethical values, and personal growth. We strive to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and character necessary to succeed and make a positive impact on society.

DepEd Recognized

Our school is officially recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd), ensuring that we meet the highest standards of quality education. This recognition affirms our commitment to providing a legitimate, credible, and well-rounded learning experience that prepares students for future success.

Class shifting schedules

Our school offers shifting schedules to provide flexible and efficient learning opportunities for students. This system ensures a well-balanced academic environment, allowing for better class management and optimized use of school resources.

Secure and Safe Environment

Our school prioritizes the safety and security of all students by maintaining a well-supervised environment, implementing strict safety protocols, and fostering a culture of discipline and respect. We are committed to providing a safe space where students can learn and grow with confidence.

FREE School Uniform

SPIC provides FREE school uniforms and allows cross-wearing, empowering students, including the LGBTQIA+ community, to express their gender with confidence.

Free PE Shirt

SPIC provides FREE PE shirts, ensuring students can stay active, comfortable, and ready to excel in their physical activities!

FREE ID & Lace

SPIC offers FREE ID and lace, ensuring students feel proud and included, while other private schools often charge for these.

Open for Block Section

SPIC offers block sectioning, allowing students to stay with their friends throughout the school year—making learning more enjoyable and collaborative.

Open for Online, Modular, & Face-to-Face

SPIC welcomes online, modular, and face-to-face students, giving you the flexibility to learn in the way that suits you best.

LGBTQIA+ Inclusive

SPIC is a safe and inclusive school that welcomes LGBTQIA+ students, ensuring everyone feels respected, valued, and free to express themselves.

ALS completers, Balik-Aral, & Private Non-ESC Completers

FREE Tuition through OVAP application (subject for approval)

ALS completers, Balik-Aral students, Private Non-ESC completers, and similar cases are eligible for FREE tuition at SPIC, provided they apply for and are granted a DepEd Voucher through the OVAP application. The school will assist students in applying for Qualified Voucher Applicant (QVA) status through Online Voucher Application Program (OVAP).

Requirements:

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Original Good Moral certificate
  • Original AF5 (ALS Form 5 if applicable)
  • Portfolio Performance Assesment
  • 2 pcs. of 2x2 ID Picture with white background

Transferees and Repeaters

FREE Tuition depending on the assessment and evaluation

Transferees and repeaters may qualify for FREE tuition at SPIC, depending on the assessment of their current situation. Any lacking subjects or missing grades may need to be enrolled separately. Eligibility will be determined based on evaluation.

Requirements:

  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Original SF9 (Grade 10 Report Card from previous school)
  • Original SF10 (Form 137 from previous school)
  • Original Good Moral Certificate
  • 2 pcs. of 2x2 ID picture with white background
  • Certificate of Transfer Credentials

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What strand do you offer?

SPIC offers ABM, GAS, Humss, Stem, TVL-ICT, and TVL-HE

Do you allow hair color and piercings?

SPIC allows hair coloring and piercings as a form of self-expression, as long as it remains neat and presentable, ensuring students look their best while embracing their individuality!

Do you have a grade requirement?

At SPIC, your potential matters more than your grades! With no grade requirement, we welcome all students, giving everyone the chance to learn, grow, and succeed in their own way!

Is there an entrance exam?

Getting into Senior High School has never been easier! At SPIC, there’s no entrance exam, no hassle—just a smooth and student-friendly enrollment process. If you're a Grade 10 completer, you're welcome here!

Do you allow students to go outside during breaktime?

SPIC has an open gate policy during break time, so students can grab their favorite snacks—whether it’s coffee, fast food, or a meal from a carinderia—at nearby establishments!

Are the classrooms at SPIC fully air-conditioned?

SPIC has fully air-conditioned classrooms, creating a cool, comfortable, and conducive environment for learning to help students focus and excel!
